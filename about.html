About ChopChains.com

Your unofficial Chop Chains resource hub

ChopChains.com is an unofficial fan site created to provide comprehensive information about Chop Chains, the exciting online puzzle game from Alexis Gelin. We aim to help players discover everything about Glitch Karts, from gameplay guides to character information and similar racing games.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create the most comprehensive and helpful resource for Chop Chains players. We provide clear information about how to play Chop Chains, time trial competitions, characters, and recommend similar puzzle games for players to enjoy.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Chop Chains gameplay guides and tips
  • Information about Chop Chains characters and their abilities
  • Time trial competition details and leaderboards
  • Curated collection of similar puzzle games
  • Regular updates with the latest Chop Chains news
